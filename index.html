Unleashing potential in People and Organizations.

Why

People excel in organizations and environments where they feel they can grow and develop themselves as they deliver results and add value to the organization.

​ Outdated organizational cultures and leadership mindsets need to shift now to create more innovative and sustainable organizations.

How

Working with the Executive Team to create clarity, alignment and the ability for them to model the way.

​ Developing leadership presence and character at the identity level to lead and transform others and the organization.

​ Building critical skills across the organization to strengthen relationship, communication and results.

What

Reframe works in deep partnership with the executive team to enable them to lead the transformation of their own organization.

We do this through intact team learning which deepens trust, relationship and commitment for organizational results.

​ We do this in a way that focuses on deep development for all individuals that results in their own individual transformations first.

ABOUT

Larry Hulsmans

Founder & Integrator

Larry has deep experience in the development of leadership in others spanning more than 25 years in the private and public sectors.   He is a Certified Presence Based coach and has degrees from the Royal Military College of Canada and Queen's University.   Larry is also a faculty member for the University of Alberta School of Business and Executive Education.

Ginette Gamache

Coach Lead

Ginette is an experienced coach, facilitator, educator, and counsellor. Ginette has a Bachelors of Education with a minor in Psychology and a Masters of Counselling Psychology. Ginette is an excellent coach with a reputation for helping people develop the insights they need to be more effective leaders and coaches.

 

OUR PARTNERS

Erin Waddell

Facilitator & Coach

As a Professional Certified Coach (PCC) and member of the International Coaching Federation, Erin focuses on practical leadership development. Either through one-on-one sessions or learning workshops in personal or virtual, Erin works with executives and leaders, helping them develop the leadership competencies needed to deliver on organizational strategies.

Mike Shaw

Experiential Lead

Mike has been an experiential facilitator  for over 25 years. He has gained a strong reputation for his ability to create an environment of trust, safety and confidence. As a member of the Association of Canadian Mountain Guides (ACMG) and Association of Challenge Course Technology (ACCT), Mike has designed and constructed a number of challenge course facilities in Western Canada.

Colin Funk

Creative Lead

Colin Funk has spent the last 25 years working as a facilitator, director and theatre producer throughout western Canada. Former director of the Leadership Learning Lab and Creativity and Innovation at The Banff Centre, Colin currently works as a creative process facilitator focussing on leadership development using experiential and creative processes.
